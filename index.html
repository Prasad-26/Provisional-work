About Us

Krishi Sarthi is an innovative idea which takes its roots from the struggles of farmers for buying the pre-requisites and selling the farms products. Agriculture being the backbone of our economy, needs to be worked out so that it is equipped with technical sustainability. This platform consists of everything a farmer requires for farming right from seeds, fertilizers to pesticides and weedicides.
         Krishi Sarthi is a bunch of professionals coming from various backgrounds working with a common aim to make agriculture sustainable through various means such as improving supply chain management, providing technical aid to the farmers, helping them to compare markets before buying or selling. With a user-friendly interface, this platform is going to help the farmers to choose their products to be bought and sold from a nationwide array of vendors. It will also help the vendors to increase their business from local to regional and more.

Vision

To assist farmers for prolific cultivation.

Mission

  • To develop a platform wherein the farmer is provided with various options to buy and sell products from local to national level.
  • To improve the supply chain from manufacturer to farmer and again from farmer to Agro-based industries thereby decreasing the middle men costs.
  • To increase the technological utilizations in farming so as to ease man power and reduce the cost.
  • To increase the customer base of vendors and farmers thereby enabling them to do business on a higher scale.
